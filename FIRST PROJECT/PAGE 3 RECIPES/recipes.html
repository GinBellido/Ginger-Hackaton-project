Huancaina Peruvian sauce

  1. In the blender, put the yellow chili paste with the oil and milk.
  2. Process with crackers, mozzarella cheese and salt to taste until its texture becomes creamy.

Cayenne Pepper Sauce

  1. In the blender, mix the cayenne pepper with 1/4 cup of water.
  2. Process with oil, salt and haf of lemon.

Madame Jeanette Sauce

  1. fry 1 onion, 4 cloves of garlic, 5 madame jeanette peppers for 10 minutes.
  2. put the mixture in a blender, add Greek yogurt, crackers and salt.
